About Coffee Café

Coffe Café offers its customers the best tasting coffee beverages in the area.
This is achieved by using high-quality ingredients and strictly following preparation guidelines. The store layout, menu listings accomadate all the varieties of coffee available today . Along with the espresso drinks, brewed coffee and teas, as well as some refreshment beverages, will be sold in the coffee bar. Coffe Café will also offer pastries, small salads and sandwiches. For the gourmet clientele that prefers to prepare its coffee at home, Java Culture will also be selling coffee beans.
